Striving for Absolute Clarity

EX - Excellent

EX (Excellent) cut diamonds represent the highest standard of craftsmanship. These diamonds are perfectly proportioned, ensuring exceptional light reflection and breathtaking sparkle. With unparalleled attention to symmetry and angles, EX-grade diamonds are the epitome of brilliance, delivering an extraordinary visual experience.

VG/G - Very Good/Good

VG (Very Good) and G (Good) diamonds at JS Diamond maintain exceptional quality with slightly relaxed proportions compared to FX. These stones still reflect a high degree of light, ensuring impressive brilliance and symmetry.

Achieving the Purest White

D-F Grade (Colourless)

D-F grade diamonds represent the pinnacle of color quality, showcasing no visible hue. These diamonds are meticulously graded on the GIA scale and are perfect for those seeking the clearest, most radiant gems.

G-J Grade (Near-Colourless)

G-J diamonds at JS Diamond strike the perfect balance between brilliance and affordability. These near-colorless gems are carefully selected to maintain their radiant appearance while offering a slightly warmer tone.

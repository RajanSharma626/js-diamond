OUR DEPARTMENT

We meticulously source our rough stones exclusively from primary miners, ensuring the utmost reliability and traceability of every diamond we produce.

Planning and Quality Control (QC)

Precision planning

Our Planning and QC department ensures every diamond is expertly analyzed and crafted to meet precise specifications.

With state-of-the-art technology and a dedicated team, we plan the transformation of rough diamonds into masterpieces. By analyzing dimensions, clarity, and potential, we maximize each diamond's brilliance and value. Our rigorous quality control process ensures that every piece exceeds customer expectations, combining precision with perfection.

Masterful Manufacturing

Crafting brilliance

At JS Diamond, our manufacturing process is a blend of artistry and innovation. Skilled artisans and cutting-edge machinery work together to bring out the best in every diamond. From cutting to polishing, each step is executed with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring the creation of timeless and breathtaking pieces.

We are committed to our ultimate objective: to craft each diamond into a masterpiece that reflects our dedication to unparalleled excellence. Every creation is a testament to our unique approach, combining traditional artistry with cutting-edge technology to deliver diamonds that are as exceptional as the moments they symbolize.

Assortment

The Assortment department classifies and grades diamonds to meet diverse client preferences and industry standards.

Our Assortment team ensures that every diamond is meticulously sorted by size, color, clarity, and cut. Using advanced grading techniques and keen expertise, we match diamonds to specific customer requirements. This process guarantees consistency, quality, and satisfaction for every client we serve.
